Banana Cream Pie



Banana Cream Pie is the best dessert ever invented and let me tell you why
Banana cream pie is the prefect combination of sweet and savory
With the filling and the frosting bringing a burst of sweetness
And a thick and savory pie crust to pull the whole thing together
Most of them even have an extra layer of bananas at the bottom
What more could you ask for?
